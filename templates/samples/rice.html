Rice University Giving Redesign

Role: Interaction Design, UI/UX Design, Web Design, Frontend, jQuery

Date: October 2014

Rice University is one of the most reputable private universities in Texas, and it was quite a treat working with their Development and Alumni Relations department on this project.
