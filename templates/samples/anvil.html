Anvil Bar & Refuge Redesign

Role: Interaction Design, UI/UX Design, Web Design, Frontend

Date: September 2014

A classic Houston staple, Anvil Bar & Refuge was one of the first bars in the city to offer classic cocktails with high quality spirits and ingredients. The idea was simple; the execution was flawless. We intended to carry this classic, simple ethos into the design of the Anvil site.
