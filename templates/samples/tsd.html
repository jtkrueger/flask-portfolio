The Simple Dollar Site & Logo Redesign

Role: UI Design, UX Design, Typography

Date: January 2013

For this project, I redesigned a finance website and its logo. One unique challenge to this project was that the site had a lot of repeat traffic, so my goal was to make the redesign look as good as possible without disrupting the experience of long-time users. Most of the changes I made were typography and white space. I also created pull-quotes and a Q&A format to give the articles a sharper, more polished look.
