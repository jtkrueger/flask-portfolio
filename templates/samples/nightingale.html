Nightingale Room Launch

Role: Interaction Design, UI/UX Design, Web Design, Project Management, Frontend, jQuery

Date: March 2015

A new concept for the restaurant group Clumsy Butcher, Nightingale Room is a Houston bar with a boozy speakeasy vibe, featuring live music and a relaxed atmosphere with premium quality drinks. We kept the design simple, utilize the venue’s photography and the classic yet forward-thinking Futura typeface. (fitting that it was created during the prohibition era of the US)

For this site, I took the lead frontend developer role, styling the site’s CSS while also managing scrolling and AJAX loading behavior for the calendar page of the site. I was also primarily in charge of the look and feel of the site, while also modeling the data and structure of the site for the backend developer. I also met with the clients to assess needs and new features, and also performed quality assurance for the site to help the launch go smoothly.
