Dynamic Slideshow on a Timeline

Role: Interaction Design, UX Design, UI Design

Date: March 2012

I was given images and content that corresponded to each slide. The project was a very open-ended, "design a slideshow." I decided to model the design based on the appearance of a timeline, since the slideshow content was very time-focused ("Today's University Is" and "Tomorrow's University Will Be").
